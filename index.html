Rolex

An unrivalled reputation
for quality and expertise

Manufacture headquartered in Geneva, Rolex is recognized for its expertise and the exceptional quality of its products.

Hans Wilsdorf, the founder of the company, envisioned a notion of perpetual excellence — a vision that led to major watchmaking innovations.

These innovations include the Oyster, the world’s first waterproof wristwatch, and the Perpetual rotor, a self-winding mechanism. The brand designs, develops, and produces the majority of its watch components in-house.

Beyond watchmaking, Rolex is deeply committed to supporting the arts and culture, sport, exploration, and global efforts to preserve the planet.

It is a responsible company whose products are made to last. In its quest for excellence, Rolex continually improves not only its watches but also its environmental footprint and impact on society.
